More about me

hi, i’m alister, a junior full stack developer with a background in digital media and web design. i started on the creative side of digital projects, working with design, content creation, and visual storytelling, and later became curious about how websites and applications work behind the scenes, which led me to development.

today, i build web applications using html, css, javascript, typescript, and angular. i enjoy breaking down problems, writing clean and maintainable code, and creating interfaces that are both functional and user-friendly.

my experience in digital projects has taught me the importance of organization, clarity, and the user perspective. i’m constantly learning, exploring new tools, and improving step by step. seeing an idea turn into an interactive, real application is what keeps me motivated.

i’m excited to collaborate on projects, share ideas, and contribute both creatively and technically.
