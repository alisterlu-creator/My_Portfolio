More about me

hi, i’m alister, a junior full stack developer with a background in digital media and web design. i originally started on the creative side of digital projects, working with design, content creation and visual storytelling. while doing that, i became more and more curious about how websites and applications actually function behind the scenes. i didn’t just want to design interfaces, i wanted to understand how everything connects and works together. that curiosity is what led me into development. today i build web applications using html, css, javascript, typescript and angular. i enjoy breaking down problems, thinking logically and structuring code in a way that stays clear and maintainable. at the same time, i care about how things look and feel, because for me functionality and user experience belong together. through my previous experience with digital projects and online platforms, i developed a good sense for organization, clarity and user perspective. i understand that building something is not just about making it work, but about making it understandable and usable. i am constantly learning, trying new tools and improving step by step. seeing an idea turn into something interactive and real is what motivates me most about working in web development.


i’d love to work together. whether you have an idea that needs building, a project that could use some creative and technical support, or just want to chat about web development, i’m always open to connecting. i enjoy collaborating, learning from others, and turning ideas into something real and usable. if that sounds like you, let’s make something together.
