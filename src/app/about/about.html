More about me

hi, i’m alister, a junior full stack developer with a background in digital media and web design. i started on the creative side of digital projects, working with design, content creation, and visual storytelling. as i went along, i became curious about how websites and applications actually work behind the scenes. i didn’t just want to design interfaces, i wanted to understand how everything connects and functions. that curiosity is what led me into development.

today i build web applications using html, css, javascript, typescript, and angular. i enjoy breaking down problems, thinking logically, and writing code that stays clear and maintainable. at the same time, i care about how things look and feel, because functionality and user experience go hand in hand.

my background in digital projects and online platforms has taught me organization, clarity, and user perspective. i know that building something isn’t just about making it work, but about making it understandable and usable. i’m constantly learning, trying new tools, and improving step by step. seeing an idea turn into something interactive and real is what keeps me motivated.

i’d love to work together. whether you have an idea that needs building, a project that could use some creative and technical support, or just want to chat about web development, i’m always open to connecting. i enjoy collaborating, learning from others, and turning ideas into something real and usable. if that sounds like you, let’s make something together.
